Doppler

This method measures the shift in the spectrum of starlight caused by the star's movement due to the gravitational pull of an orbiting planet. This shift indicates the star's velocity, which suggests the presence of a planet.

Astrometri

Metode ini mengukur perubahan kecil pada posisi Bintang dilangit akibat tarikan gravitasi planet yang mengorbit.

Transit

This method measures small changes in the position of a star in the sky due to the gravitational pull of an orbiting planet.

Micro Gravity

This method observes the bending of light from a background star by the gravitational field of a foreground star. If a planet is orbiting the foreground star, it can alter the lensing effect, indicating the presence of the planet.
